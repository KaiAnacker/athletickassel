## Register

**brand** — this is a marketing site. The design *is* the product; a visitor's
impression is the thing being made. Goal: a Kassel athlete books a trial.

## Product purpose

City Athletic Kassel is the training studio of **Christian Tripp**, a personal and
Hyrox coach in central Kassel (Kurfürstenstraße 10). It offers three things:

1. **Hyrox preparation** — targeted training for the 8 km / 8-station fitness race.
2. **Gruppentraining** — small-group classes in three focuses, priority-ordered: **Hyrox** (1),
   **TRX & Functional** (2), then **general fitness & strength** (HIIT, Langhantel, Athletik,
   Mobility) (3). Booked via Eversports.
3. **Personal Training (1:1)** — measured coaching via the oneathlete platform:
   baseline test, plan, retest, documented progress.

The single idea under all of it: **time and measurement**. Hyrox is one total time.
Personal training is baseline → retest. Progress is numbers, not a feeling.

## Users

- **The Hyrox-curious athlete** (25–45): already trains, wants structure and a number
  to beat. Reads "8 stations" and knows exactly what that means.
- **The returner / beginner** who wants intense-but-guided small-group training and a
  free trial before committing.
- **The 1:1 client** who wants accountability and measurable change (race time, 1RM,
  body composition).

They are German-speaking, in and around Kassel, and they evaluate on credibility:
real results, real reviews (4.9 on Eversports), a real coach.

## Brand voice

Three physical-object words: **race-clock, chalk-and-steel, stamped-concrete**.
Precise, gritty, kinetic. Confident without shouting. Speaks in splits and numbers.

- Direct, second person ("Deine Zeit", "Komm vorbei").
- Quantitative: minutes, metres, reps, PRs, spots free.
- German, natural and warm, never corporate fitness-cliché ("Beste Version deiner selbst").
- No hype it can't back with a number.

## Anti-references (what to avoid)

- Generic gym-chain site: stock smiles, blue gradients, "Jetzt durchstarten!".
- Editorial-magazine cosplay: display-serif + italic drop caps. This brand is a race
  protocol, not a literary journal. (The first concept drifted here; we corrected it.)
- Wellness-spa softness. This is sport: effort, sweat, a clock.

## Strategic principles

- **Numbers are heroes.** Every section should expose a measurable thing.
- **One total time.** The race-timing metaphor is the spine; sections are splits.
- **Booking is the conversion.** Eversports (groups) and oneathlete (1:1) are the two
  doors; both must always be one tap away.
- **Local and real.** Kassel, Kurfürstenstraße 10, a named coach, real reviews.
